Meghan's 19th Birthday

Meghan's birthday was a great time. Early on in the day Avery and I invited her over to suprise her with a bottle of champagne, balloons, and gifts. I bought her some of her favourite makeup, and Avery got her a Zach Bryan flag. In the evening, we went out to the bar Kentucky in Waterloo. We shared good drinks, spent some time with the DJ, and met some new people. It was a good night.
